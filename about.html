About me

I'm Yiming Teng (滕一鸣), and you?

I'm currently an undergraduate student in School of Physics, Peking University. My research interest lies in scattering amplitudes and primodial cosmology, particularly quantum field theory in dS (de Sitter) spacetime and cosmological bootstrap, and I've been studying/researching in this field supervised by Zhong-Zhi Xianyu (鲜于中之) at Department of Physics, Tsinghua University. By September 2023, I'll be on an official one-semester undergraduate exchange program to Niels Bohr Institute, University of Copenhagen.

Besides a passion for theoretical physics, I'm also a fanatical fan of classical music (especially symphonies by Mahler and Shostakovich), history and philosophy of science as well as linguistics. Moreover, I'm a licensed amateur radio operator in China.

Study

Here are selected notes I wrote, most of which are in Chinese and some of them are incomplete.

Name Language Description
Lie Groups and Lie Algebras English Incomplete note on Lie groups and Lie algebras, lectures given by Prof Yinan Wang in the spring semester, 2022.
Electrodynamics Chinese Note on electrodynamics, lectures given by Prof Xiongjun Liu during 2021 fall semester. Prof Liu's pedagogy is revolutionary, making the course into an excellent de facto introduction to classical field theory.
Quantum Field Theory and the Standard Model Chinese Incomplete note taken during my reading on Quantum Field Theory and the Standard Model by Matthew D. Schwartz, essentially a Chinese translation of the original book with annotation.
The Geometry of Physics Chinese Incomplete note taken during my reading on The Geometry of Physics by Theodore Frankel.
Early Development of Quantum Field Theory Chinese Originally a course report of atomic physics lectured by Prof Renxin Xu during 2022 fall semester, which can also be considered as a concised overall introduction to quantum field theory in the 1920s and early 30s.
Decoding TETRA with RTL-SDR dongle Chinese A detailed instruction on my interesting project using RTL-SDR dongle with GNU Radio to receive and decode TETRA (terrestrial trunked radio) signal.
